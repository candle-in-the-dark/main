Candle In The Dark

Game Description

In this game you must navigate a deep dark with only the light generated by your candle to guide you. If you take too long you will surely starve but if you find the archway you will have a chance to earn your freedom. If you complete the dragon's riddle you may walk free, if not you'll be burned alive!

Controls

To navigate the maze simply hold down an arrow key in the direction you want to move. Upon completing the riddle you will be presented with a high scores page logging the accomplishments of other users.

Registered Play

There is also an Epic Quest that only registered users can play. Create an account and click "Epic Quest" to play! You will explore three mazes in sequence, confronting the dragon each time. But if you win you will live forever in the hall of fame, as well as see a page very few explorers can find.
